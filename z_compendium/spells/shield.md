# Shield
*1st-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 reaction
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from [magic missile](magic-missile.md).

**Classes**: [Artificer (Artillerist)](artificer-artillerist-tce.md), [Artificer (Battle Smith)](artificer-battle-smith-tce.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Sorcerer](sorcerer.md), [Warlock (The Hexblade)](warlock-the-hexblade-xge.md), [Wizard](wizard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 275. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
