# Darkvision
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate
- **Duration:** 8 hour

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has [darkvision](_senses.md#darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet.

**Classes**: [Artificer](artificer-tce.md), [Druid](druid.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Monk (Way of Shadow)](monk-way-of-shadow.md), [Ranger](ranger.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Sorcerer](sorcerer.md), [Wizard](wizard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 230. Available in the SRD.*
