# Heartstone
*Other, very rare*  


This lustrous black gem allows a [night hag](2.%20GM%20Tools/5eTools%20Compendium%20&%20Rules/z_compendium/bestiary/fiend/b_night-hag.md) to become ethereal while it is in her possession. The touch of a heartstone also cures any disease. Crafting a heartstone takes 30 days.

*Source: Monster Manual p. 179*
